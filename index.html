Privacy Policy for FormatPro

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

FormatPro is designed with a privacy-first approach. We respect your data and ensure complete transparency in how information is handled.

1. Data Collection

FormatPro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

All data entered (such as JSON, XML, or SQL text) is processed locally within your browser.

2. Data Usage

User-provided input is used only for formatting and improving readability.

  • No tracking of user activity
  • No monitoring of browsing history
  • No collection of personal information

3. Data Sharing

FormatPro does not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

4. Permissions

The extension may request limited permissions (such as clipboard access) strictly to support core functionality like copying formatted output.

No data is stored or transmitted during this process.

5. Security

Since FormatPro operates entirely within the browser, your data never leaves your device. This eliminates risks associated with external data storage.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected with a revised effective date.
